Proposal Assignment

Scrolling Less, Studying More: A mobile-based productivity tracking system designed to improve student focus and academic efficiency.

This project examines how real-time tracking of screen time, app usage, and study duration can influence student behavior. The goal is to reduce distractions and improve productivity by giving students immediate feedback during study sessions.

The system tracks study time, detects distractions, and alerts users when they switch to non-productive apps. It is based on behavioral self-regulation and cognitive load theory, showing how real-time feedback can improve focus.

Testing will involve students using the app for two weeks while tracking improvements in focus and study habits. The expected result is improved time management and reduced distractions.
