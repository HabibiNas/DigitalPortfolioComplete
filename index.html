Proposal Assignment

This proposal identifies a common problem faced by students and presents a structured solution. The assignment emphasizes organization, clarity, and professional communication. In this revised version, I focused on making the problem, solution, and benefits easier to understand.

Improving Student Productivity Through a Real-Time Study Space Finder

Students at large universities often struggle to find available study spaces because many campus resources do not provide live information about crowding, noise levels, or available seating. This can waste time and make it harder for students to stay productive during busy parts of the day.

The proposed solution is a real-time study space finder that allows students to check study locations before walking across campus. The platform would show live occupancy, noise levels, available amenities, and alternative locations when one area is full.

This solution would help students save time, reduce frustration, and find better places to study. By giving students clearer information, the university can improve the overall student experience and support academic success.
